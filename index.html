Stefan Lovatt

I'm a digital art designer based in Adelaide, Australia. I love re-creating other peoples most loved vehicles into a digital art format. Whether it's a cherished vintage car, or an ambitious dream, you name it, I’ll draw it. Please take some time going through some of the digital art work that I’ve created, and some custom designs requested by my customers. My art work can also be viewed on Redbubble and Instagram. Come join me on the official Turismo-ink Facebook page where i run competitions and give you a chance to win your car being drawn. If you are interested in having a custom image done, feel free to contact me on the contact form below.
